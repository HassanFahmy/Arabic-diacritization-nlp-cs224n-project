Resource
===========
Classical Arabic:
- Arabic al-Shamela library 2011-Jun-21.
- 97 Books filtered from 7079 books from  Shamela Library.
Modern Standard Arabic
- 20 modern books
- Texts crawled from Internet
- learning.aljazeera.net
- al-kalema.org
- enfal.de 
- diverse ...
- Manually diacritized
